I am a PhD student under the supervision of Marc Aiguier and Gilles Dowek, in the Logimics team at MICS laboratory and in the Deducteam at Laboratoire Méthodes Formelles.

As a student, I was part of the research curriculum of CentraleSupélec, under the supervision of Valentin Blot and Gilles Dowek, at Laboratoire Méthodes Formelles. My work was focused on the λΠ-calculus modulo theory and the Lambdapi proof assistant. In particular, I implemented an encoding of set theory in Lambdapi. Working with Théo Winterhalter, I studied the replacement of rewrite rules by axioms inside the λΠ-calculus modulo theory.

Research interests
