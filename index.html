Thomas Traversié


I am a PhD student under the supervision of Marc Aiguier and Gilles Dowek, in the MICS laboratory at CentraleSupélec and in the Deducteam at Laboratoire Méthodes Formelles.

As a student, I was part of the research curriculum of CentraleSupélec, in the Deducteam at Laboratoire Méthodes Formelles, under the supervision of Valentin Blot and Gilles Dowek. In particular, I implemented an encoding of set theory in Dedukti.


Research interests

⊢ Logical frameworks
⊢ Type theory
⊢ Rewrite rules
